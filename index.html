Self-Introduction

I am a student who is passionate about using computers and the arts to explore healing. My artwork attempts to provide the viewer with a therapeutic experience by employing colour, texture, and sound to evoke a sense of calm and peace. In some ways, I think my work can aid individuals in getting over the stress and difficulties of daily life since it completely immerses the viewer in the world of digital art. I can also experience the emotions from everyday life that digital art has infused into the interactive experience. I want to use the careful calculation of facts to provide visual impact and emotional resonance. Whether it's a complex digital landscape or a straightforward abstract piece.
